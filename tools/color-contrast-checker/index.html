Understanding WCAG Standards

WCAG AA - Normal Text

Minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 required for regular text (under 18pt or 14pt bold).

WCAG AA - Large Text

Minimum contrast ratio of 3:1 required for large text (18pt+ or 14pt+ bold).

WCAG AAA - Normal Text

Enhanced contrast ratio of 7:1 required for the highest accessibility level.

WCAG AAA - Large Text

Enhanced contrast ratio of 4.5:1 required for large text at AAA level.
